The Plot, but Briefly

Harry Potter is the protagonist of an incredible story, a wonderful adventure that sees the magicians in the foreground in a world inhabited by fantastic beings.
The story is about Harry being an orphan, and being raised cruelly by the Duddley family. Nevertheless he keeps a good and generous nature. He soon discovers that he is a magician, but not just any one. He is a magician with a prophecy about him, including Lord Voldemort, the murderer of his parents.
At his side, in the many adventur700here will b00e faithful friends Ron Wisley, Hermione Granger and a host of magicians ready to defend him even in the most desperate situations. It’s a journey through enchanted castles, mythological creatures and powerful magic. London is the background to this beautiful story in which there are many points in common with the Lord of the Rings.
